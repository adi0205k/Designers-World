Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Here's a general breakdown:

  • Basic website (5 pages): 2-3 weeks
  • E-commerce website: 4-6 weeks
  • Brand identity package: 4-6 weeks
  • Social media management: Ongoing service
  • Video production: 2-4 weeks per video

We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation, and we always keep you updated on progress throughout the project.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages to keep your digital assets running smoothly:

  • Regular security updates and backups
  • Content updates and management
  • Performance optimization
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Monthly analytics reports
  • SEO maintenance

Our maintenance packages are available on a monthly or annual basis, with different tiers to suit your needs and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We offer multiple payment options to make it convenient for our clients:

  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Bank Transfers (NEFT/RTGS)
  • International Wire Transfers

For larger projects, we offer flexible payment plans with milestone-based payments. A 50% advance payment is required to start the project, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-launch support to ensure your project's success:

  • 30 days of free support after launch
  • Bug fixes and technical issues resolution
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Security updates and maintenance
  • Content updates and management
  • Training sessions for your team

After the initial 30-day period, you can choose from our various support packages that include different levels of service and response times to match your needs.

Can I make changes to my website after it's launched?

Yes, you have several options for making changes to your website:

  • Content Management System (CMS) access for basic updates
  • Regular maintenance packages for ongoing changes
  • On-demand support for specific updates
  • Training sessions to learn how to make updates yourself

We'll provide you with a user-friendly CMS that allows you to make basic content updates without technical knowledge. For more complex changes, our team is always available to help.

What is your design process?

Our design process follows these key steps:

  • Initial consultation to understand your needs
  • Research and strategy development
  • Concept creation and initial designs
  • Client feedback and revisions
  • Final design approval
  • Implementation and launch

We maintain clear communication throughout the process and ensure your vision is accurately translated into the final design.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive hosting solutions:

  • Shared hosting for small to medium websites
  • VPS hosting for larger projects
  • Cloud hosting for high-traffic websites
  • SSL certificates for security
  • Regular backups and monitoring
  • 24/7 technical support

Our hosting packages include free migration from your current host and come with various features to ensure optimal performance and security.

What about SEO and digital marketing?

We offer comprehensive digital marketing services:

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Content Marketing
  • Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising
  • Email Marketing
  • Analytics and Reporting

Our digital marketing strategies are tailored to your business goals and include regular performance reports and optimization.
